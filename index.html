What Is Water Damage Restoration Service?

Water damage restoration is a professional process that involves cleaning, drying, sanitizing, and repairing a property that has been affected by water. Water can severely damage flooring, walls, furniture, and even the structural integrity of your building.

A qualified water damage restoration service typically includes:

Emergency response and inspection

Water extraction

Dehumidifying and drying

Mold remediation

Cleaning and sanitizing

Structural repairs and reconstruction
